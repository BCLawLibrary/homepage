The Teaching & Research Librarians are legal experts available to help you with your research. Ask them about:

Research Training
Hone your legal research skills for class, clinic assignments, and the workplace. Research training sessions include: 
  • Researching cases, statues, administrative law, and secondary sources
  • Understanding legal citations and The Bluebook
  • Locating and using study aids
  • Using practical guidance tools, dockets, and more
